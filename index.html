About Us

本中心提供儿童青少年心理与成长，心理咨询、家庭建设、婚姻咨询、移民适应、跨国婚姻等咨询与心理治疗。具体形式有个体咨询、家庭咨询和团体咨询。

We provide children and adolescence counseling, psychotherapy, family building, marriage counseling, immigrant adaption, and cross-cultural marriage counseling.
